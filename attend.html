Who Should Join?

Anyone interested about surviving.

Come and try out if you have:

  • Loyalty
  • Strength
  • Commitment

What you will learn:

    • Pistols/Derringers
    • Rifles
    • Shotguns
  2. Close-quarters combat

Consequences of unruly behavior:

  • Burn one side of your face for ten seconds
  • Depending on the severity, your head will get 100% smashed by Lucille

Tier 2:

  • M16

  • AK-47
  • MP5

Officers (all weapons given):

  • AK-74
